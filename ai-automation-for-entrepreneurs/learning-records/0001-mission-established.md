# Mission established: ship one real AI automation, for non-technical founders

The instructor is designing this course **for** non-technical entrepreneurs (not
learning it themselves). The agreed mission: by course end, each learner has
**automated one real workflow in their own business** with AI. Delivery is hybrid
(self-paced HTML lessons + weekly live build sessions), right-sized to **4 weeks,
~3–4 hrs/week, 12 lessons + 4 live sessions**.

Why this steers future sessions:
- Every lesson must produce one tangible win tied to the learner's *own* workflow — abstract/theory-only lessons are off-mission.
- The audience abandons on jargon and length; lessons stay ≤15 min and lead with payoff.
- Tooling leans no-code (Zapier/Make); coding, fine-tuning, and self-hosting are explicitly out of scope.
- Lessons are built just-in-time in the zone of proximal development, not all at once. 0001 + glossary are built as the design standard.
