# Mission: AI Workflow Automation for Non-Technical Entrepreneurs

## Why
Non-technical founders lose hours every week to repetitive work — chasing leads,
answering the same customer questions, copying data between apps, writing routine
content. This class exists so that by the end, each entrepreneur has **automated at
least one real workflow in their own business** with AI, freeing time and money to
spend on the work only they can do.

## Success looks like
- A learner can describe, in plain language, what an AI model is good and bad at — and decide where it belongs in their business.
- A learner can write a reliable, reusable prompt for a recurring business task (not a one-off lucky result).
- A learner can build a working no-code automation (e.g. in Zapier or Make) that triggers on a real event and takes an AI-powered action.
- Each learner ships **one capstone automation** that runs in their actual business by the end of the course.

## Constraints
- **Audience:** non-technical — no coding, comfortable with everyday SaaS (email, spreadsheets, a CRM).
- **Time:** busy founders. Target ~3–4 hrs/week, 4 weeks. Self-paced lessons are short (10–15 min each).
- **Delivery:** hybrid — self-paced HTML lessons during the week + one live office-hours / build session per week.
- **Budget:** assume free or low-cost tiers of tools where possible; flag when a paid tier is needed.

## Out of scope (for this course)
- Writing code, APIs, or self-hosting (n8n self-host, custom dev).
- Training or fine-tuning models.
- Deep prompt-engineering theory beyond what's needed to get reliable business results.
- Choosing/standing up a CRM or core business systems from scratch.
