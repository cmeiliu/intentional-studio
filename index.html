How we'll work

A short, honest process.

01

Discovery call

Free, 30 minutes, no pitch deck. We figure out whether this is even worth doing.

02

03

Short cycles

2–3 week sprints with something usable at the end of each. You see progress, you give notes.

04

Hand it back

Clean code, clear docs, and a team that can carry it forward without me. No lock-in.
